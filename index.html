Greetings and welcome to my homepage!

My name, as you might've figured out by now, is pixelqiwi, and I do things. Usually when I'm bored or feeling unnormally creative.

I enjoy making music, editing videos and just having fun really.

I've been doing this for a decade now, and for me, there's no better feeling than finishing a long-ass project that you've been working on for a long time, be that hours, days, months, or even years. It's so euphorical it's crazy.

For the sake of easy access I have made the navigation bar.

  • In the "social" dropdown you can find most of the social network links that I use. Feel free to contact me on any of them!

  • In the "video" dropdown I have my Youtube channel and my channels on various streaming services.

  • In the "audio" dropdown I listed all my SoundCloud pages where I might post my music or anything else.

  • If you're feeling generous, you might find the "donate" dropdown useful.

  • The "minecraft" dropdown houses links to various Minecraft projects I'm currently working on.

  • The "misc" dropdown has all the pages that didn't make it into any of the dropdowns listed above.

    • And that's about it, thank you for visiting my page!
    Have a good day!
